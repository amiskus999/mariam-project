About

Behind The Scenes

I didn't grown up wanting to be an architect. I had other plans. Yet still I always wanted to understand how things were constructed, how they were joined together. I made observations, asked questions but I didn't have answers to them. Guess my thoughts and questions still needed to be answered as I find myself pursuing the course to make me understand them in Kwame Nkrumah University of Science And Technology as an Architecture Student. As Eileen Gray said" to create, one must first question everything."I believe my inquisitive nature has brought me up to create my own designs and learn how those designs can be brought to life. If a brick wants to be something, can't I also be an architect?
